Ontario Airport

For the past three decades CK3, LLC has provided Engineering and Surveying to the City of Ontario in an effort to improve the Ontario Airport. These efforts will bring more business to our community.

Current:
CK3 has been working closely with JUB Engineers on the City of Ontario Airport Project in an effort to provide better services and bring more business to our community. CK3 provided the topographical survey to JUB for the design. The topographical survey included all the utilities, runway and major land features at the Airport. This Project will provide the airport with a new storm system, new reconstructed apron, newly paved taxiways, and a repaired and resurfaced runway.

2007:
Working closely with Roger Smith and the City of Ontario CK3 provided Structural Engineering calculations for the City of Ontario Airport sign.

2007 / 2006:
In 2006 CK3 performed a topographic survey for Toothman Orton Engineering Company, Toothman Orton designed the new runway and taxiway extension which lengthened the runway to just over 5000 feet. This extension allows the Airport to service larger planes here in Ontario and bring more business to our community. After the topographical survey in 2006, CK3 followed up with the construction surveying of the extended runway and taxiway in 2007. We worked closely with Mid Valley Construction on this project. In addition to the new runway and taxiways, this job also consisted of a new storm system at the extended end and fencing the entire airport property.

2003:
CK3 provided the topographic survey to Toothman Orton Engineering Company for the designing of the parking lot paving, fence installation and general paving repair and crack filling.

1998:
CK3 provided Contract Documents and Contract Document support to the City of Ontario Airport.

1989:
Working closely with Century West Engineering and Ontario Asphalt, CK3 provided construction surveying. This project consisted of pavement rehabilitation on the runway, taxiway and apron areas along with correcting drainage issues and golf course improvements.

1981:
CK3 provided Civil Engineering for the Airport Industrial Park area. These are all very good projects to be a part of when it comes to helping the community grow.
