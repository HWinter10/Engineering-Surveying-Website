company history

CK3, LLC dates back to 1972 with its parent company being "G&W Engineering". The company has had several name changes over the years and had become known as "Edwards & Cummings, LLC". In 2006, by the merger of the two local firms "Edwards & Cummings, LLC" and "K-3 Services, Inc." the firm became known as "CK3, LLC." Click here to learn more about the company's history.

Each location's history :

ontario office


Our Ontario Office, originally located at 34 N. Oregon Street, was established in 1972 by the original principles Albert Gaschler and Bill Weaver. In 1985 the office relocated to 768 Sunset Drive and in 1997 to 156 SE 1st Street in Ontario. We moved to our current building, located at 368 SW 5th Avenue, in 2007. Ontario is our main office where we manage billings and administrative work. The office can be reached at: (541)-889-5411.


hermiston office


Our Hermiston Office (originally known as Gaschler & Associates) was established in 1975 and was originally located in the Reclamation Building in Hermiston, Oregon. Albert Gaschler, David Leonard and Denny Edwards were the original principles in the Hermiston Office. In 1987 we relocated to 82077 Hwy 395 N., in Umatilla, Oregon. We moved into our current building, located at 945 W. Orchard Avenue and locally known as the Orchard Pharmacy, in 2011. The Hermiston office can be reached at: (541)-567-2345.
