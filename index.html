welcome

CK3 is a Professional Engineering, Surveying and Planning firm with offices located in Ontario and Hermiston, Oregon. We specialize in Civil, Structural and Electrical Engineering, as well as Boundary, Construction and GPS Surveying and Land Development Planning. We have provided services to clients all over the globe and continue to serve clients across the greater Northwest.
